Trinary listener

Hi! I will ask you did you do it and then listen your answer. I will repeat what I understood before I begin to listen you again.

Begin our conversation by pressing "Start talk" button below the list. Your browser will ask you a permission to access your microphone.

Google Chrome 33 or greater is required. Also ensure both your microfone and speakers are working and loud enough.

Commands:
yes, okay - feed 1
maybe, unknown - feed 0
no, negative - feed -1
stop - stop listening
